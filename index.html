FRONTAL WINGLETS

Design followed by function which is to increase the downforce of the front axle of the motorbike and improve the coefficient of aerodynamic penetration, being the first part of the motorcycle to impact with the air. This downforce will provide an anti-wheelie effect, more stability when braking, anti-shimmie, and more stability at high speeds.

LEVER PROTECTORS – REARVIEW MIRRORS

A new product we designed for this amazing Naked+ bike is a multifunctional one, a lever protector that also works as a rearview mirror. Made in machined aluminium, this part has a lower position that works as a lever protector, with an aero-wing shape that will cut the air in a clean manner, like an airplane wing does. And the upper position allows us to rotate the wing and we discover a mirror underneath that can be regulated to the exact position needed.

ENGINE SPOILER

The Diablo deserved an engine spoiler that completed and gave an optimal aerodynamic volume to the engine & surrounding components. With the addition of this new texture, we achieve to accelerate the air that flows between the bike and the ground, and by doing so the pressure will decreases, and this will translate into more vertical load for the motorcycle.

REAR SPOILER

The new DiabloX incorporates this spoiler right before the rear tyre. Its main function is to create vertical load directly on the swingarm, not the chassis. The rotation of the tire generates an additional load by spinning air closer to the tire, right against this spoiler. Creating also downforce on the rear axle of the motocycle, thus balancing the bikes charges.

SIDE COVERS

The new side covers have achieved a new style based on aerodynamics and air flowing. With a softer and aerodynamically optimized design, also following the motorcycles existing design lines and the rear seat cowl forms. They allow the regulation of the rear suspension without having to be removed to do so.

NEW SEAT DESIGN

In collaboration with Luimoto, we designed this special edition seat design that completes the amazing look of this concept motorbike. With an engraved DiabloX logo and different textures, it becomes a special part of the concept design.
